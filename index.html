Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers, so you can feel grounded before you reach out.

Where is your office located?

The office is located at 1955 Merrick Road, Suite 205A, Merrick, New York 11566.

What if my child is hesitant to start therapy or to come to the office?

We prioritize a warm, comfortable environment and take time to build trust. Many children are slow to warm at first. Our team brings broad experience from schools, camps, and family settings and uses kid friendly approaches to make the process feel safer and more engaging.

Do you accept insurance?

The practice is not in network with insurance panels. A superbill can be provided after payment for potential out of network reimbursement. Please confirm benefits, deductibles, and reimbursement rates with your insurance company in advance.

How long is a typical therapy session or evaluation session?

Therapy sessions are typically 45 minutes. Evaluation length varies based on the testing plan and is commonly completed across 2 to 3 visits.

What is the ADOS 2?

ADOS 2 stands for Autism Diagnostic Observation Schedule, 2nd Edition. It is a structured evaluation used alongside other measures to support diagnostic decisions about autism spectrum disorder. It is rarely used in isolation and is typically one part of a comprehensive evaluation.

How long will evaluations take?

Depending on what testing is needed, evaluations often take around 4 weeks from start to finish, including information gathering, testing, report writing, and a feedback session.

What is your rate for services?

Fees are provided by phone or email based on the service and clinician. If you do not have insurance or are not using insurance, you can request a Good Faith Estimate before scheduling.
