We create a warm, comfortable, and accepting environment for all ages. Establishing a trusting rapport is essential, and children can be slow to warm. We incorporate kid friendly modalities such as games and gentle incentives to help the experience feel safe and rewarding.

What is the ADOS-2?
ADOS-2 stands for Autism Diagnostic Observation Schedule, 2nd Edition. It is a structured measure used alongside other tools as part of a comprehensive autism evaluation and is rarely used in isolation.
How long will evaluations take?
Evaluations often take around 4 weeks from start to finish, including information gathering, testing, report writing, and the feedback session.
