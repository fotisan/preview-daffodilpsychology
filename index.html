Frequently asked questions

Clear answers, upfront, so you feel confident about next steps.

Where is your office located?
The office is located at 1955 Merrick Road, Suite 205A, Merrick, New York 11566.
What if my child is hesitant to start therapy or to come to the office?
We create a warm, comfortable, and accepting environment for all ages. Building a trusting rapport is essential, and children can be slow to warm. Our team uses kid friendly modalities such as games and gentle incentives to help the experience feel safe and rewarding.
Do you accept insurance?
We are not members of insurance panels at this time. After payment, we provide a superbill for potential out of network reimbursement. Please check your plan‚Äôs mental health benefits and deductible details in advance.
How long is a typical therapy session or evaluation session?
Therapy sessions are typically 45 minutes. Evaluation timing varies based on testing needs and often takes place over 2‚Äì3 visits, followed by a feedback session.
What is the ADOS-2?
ADOS-2 stands for Autism Diagnostic Observation Schedule, 2nd Edition. It is a structured assessment used alongside other measures to support diagnostic clarity. It is rarely used in isolation and is usually part of a comprehensive evaluation.
How long will evaluations take?
Evaluations often take around four weeks from start to finish, including information gathering, testing, report writing, and the feedback session, all included in the evaluation fee.
What is your rate for services?
Fees are shared by phone or email for the specific service. If you are not using insurance, you can request a Good Faith Estimate at least one business day before a scheduled service.
