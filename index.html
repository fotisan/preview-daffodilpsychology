What if my child is hesitant to start therapy or to come to the office?
We make every effort to create a warm, comfortable, and accepting environment for all ages. Establishing a trusting rapport is essential. Children can be slow to warm, so we incorporate kid friendly modalities, including games and gentle incentives, to soften the experience.
Do you accept insurance?
At this time, we are not members of insurance panels. Upon payment, a superbill is provided for potential out of network reimbursement. Please check your plan‚Äôs benefits and deductible details in advance.
How long is a typical therapy session or evaluation session?
Therapy sessions are usually 45 minutes. Evaluation length varies based on testing needs, often across 2‚Äì3 visits, followed by a feedback session.
What is the ADOS-2?
ADOS-2 stands for Autism Diagnostic Observation Schedule, 2nd Edition. It is a structured measure used with other tools to support diagnostic clarity as part of a broader, comprehensive evaluation.
How long will evaluations take?
Evaluations often take around 4 weeks from start to finish, including information gathering, testing, report writing, and the feedback session.
What is your rate for services?
Fees are shared by phone and or email based on the specific service. You can request a Good Faith Estimate at least 1 business day before your service if you are not using insurance.
